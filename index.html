QR-Based Emergency Safety & Contact System

About Raksha Tag

Raksha Tag is a public service initiative to help people stay safe in emergency situations. Our QR-based ID tags link to emergency contact details, helping good samaritans or authorities contact family instantly.

How It Works

Each Raksha Tag carries a unique QR code. When scanned, it displays pre-linked emergency contact information of the person it belongs to. Useful for children, senior citizens, travelers, and vehicle owners.
